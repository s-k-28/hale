Privacy Policy

Effective June 12, 2026 · Applies to the HALE mobile app and haleapp.com link pages

HALE helps you quit nicotine. To do that, the app stores sensitive information about your quit journey. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, who processes it on our behalf, and how to delete it. We never sell your data, and we never use your health information for advertising or marketing.

What we collect

  • Account. HALE creates an anonymous account by default — no name or email is required. You may optionally add a first name and, later, an email to save your progress.
  • Quit profile & health logs. The product you're quitting, daily baseline and cost, triggers, hardest hour, quit date, check-ins, streaks, craving logs (time, intensity, trigger, outcome), and relapse logs. This is health and wellness data; we treat it with the strictest handling below.
  • Sage chat. Messages you exchange with Sage, our AI quit coach.
  • Community content. Posts, comments, and reactions you publish under a system-generated pseudonym, plus reports and blocks you file.
  • Buddy & referral data. Buddy pairings, nudges, and referral codes/attribution when you share or use an invite link.
  • Purchases. Subscription status (via Apple/Google billing — we never see your payment details).
  • Usage & diagnostics. In-app events (screens used, features tapped) with coarse quit-journey context, push notification tokens, and crash reports.

How your health data is used

Your quit profile, health logs, and chat content are used only to run the app: tracking your progress, personalizing your plan and Sage's coaching, and powering the features you use. They are never used for advertising, marketing, or sold or shared with data brokers, and never stored in iCloud. HALE shows no third-party ads and does not track you across other companies' apps or websites.

Your choices and consent

  • AI chat consent. Sage is off until you explicitly agree, in the app, to share chat data with the AI providers above. Decline and the rest of HALE still works. You can withdraw this consent anytime in You ▸ Settings ▸ "AI coach data sharing" — Sage pauses immediately and no further chat data is shared.
  • Analytics. Withdraw anytime: You ▸ Settings ▸ "Share anonymous analytics."
  • Notifications. Controlled by the in-app prompt and iOS/Android settings.

Deletion and retention

Delete your account anytime in the app: You ▸ Settings ▸ Delete account. This permanently erases your account, quit history, health logs, chats, community content, and buddy/referral records from our database, and triggers deletion of your records at OneSignal, PostHog, and RevenueCat. Deletion is immediate and irreversible. Deleting your account does not cancel an active App Store subscription — manage that with Apple. We retain data only while your account exists.

Security

Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest by our hosting providers. Session credentials are stored in your device's secure keychain. Community pseudonyms are never publicly linked to your account.

Children

HALE is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age in your region), and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

We'll update this page and the effective date when this policy changes; material changes are announced in the app.
